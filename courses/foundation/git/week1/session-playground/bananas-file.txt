Bananas are one of the most popular fruits in the world. 
They are naturally sweet and full of energy, which makes them a perfect snack during the day.
Bananas are also rich in potassium, a mineral that helps keep our heart healthy and our muscles strong.
Many people enjoy bananas on their own, in smoothies, or with breakfast meals like oatmeal or pancakes. 
They are easy to carry, affordable, and healthy.

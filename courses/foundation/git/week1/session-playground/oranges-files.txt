Oranges are juicy and refreshing fruits that are enjoyed all over the world. 
They are an excellent source of vitamin C, which helps strengthen the immune system and keeps our skin healthy. 
Oranges grow on trees and are usually harvested in warm regions. 
People often eat them fresh, drink them as orange juice, or use them in desserts and salads. 
The bright color and sweet taste make oranges a favorite fruit for many.
